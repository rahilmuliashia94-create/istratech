Company Profile & History

Established in 2003, Everest Security Pvt. Ltd. has been delivering turnkey online monitoring and protection solutions across India. Our mission is to provide reliable, professional and technically advanced security systems built on proven Israeli technology and best practices from global vendors.

Over the past two decades we have installed and managed systems for a wide spectrum of clients — from large financial organizations and government entities to retail showrooms, factories and private residences. We focus on high availability monitoring: 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our services cover full lifecycle security: consultancy, system design, supply of equipment, professional installation, training, and continuous monitoring and maintenance. We emphasize fast emergency response, precise detection algorithms and reliable communications back to our control center.

Technology partners and product lines include advanced alarm panels, GPRS/4G vehicle trackers, NVRs, AI-enabled video analytics, smoke and fire detection systems and panic button integrations for mission-critical locations like banks and ATMs.
