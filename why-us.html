Enterprise-grade control & redundant reporting

We use professional control panels with multiple reporting channels (IP, GSM backup). Each panel is configured with site-specific rules and remote management so we can diagnose or update systems without unnecessary site visits.

This reduces downtime and keeps your monitoring live even when the primary connection fails.

Perimeter and exterior detection

For yards, logistics sites and large properties we install perimeter detection arrays, calibrated for local weather and vegetation. Perimeter sensors trigger camera sweeps, lighting, and immediate operator verification, catching incidents before they reach valuable assets.

These systems significantly reduce theft and unauthorised access at scale.

Smart detection & false-alarm reduction

We combine motion, environmental sensors and optional video analytics so the system tells us when there is a real problem, not when normal activity happens. Settings are tuned by site, shift and expected traffic patterns to avoid nuisance alerts.

The result: fewer interruptions, lower operational cost, and higher confidence in response actions.
